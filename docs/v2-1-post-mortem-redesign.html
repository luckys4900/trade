§1 Failure Decomposition

1.1 What the Numbers Reveal

REPORTED: trades=216, return=-0.1348%, WR=44.91%, E=-0.0001%/trade CRITICAL OBSERVATION: Total $ loss = $10k × 0.1348% = only $13.48 Per trade avg = -$0.062 on $30k position = -0.0002% This is NOT a series of TP/SL hits. This is 216 trades exiting at ~ZERO P&L (timeout). IF TP=0.40% and SL=0.25% hit at WR=44.9%: 97 wins × $120 - 119 losses × $75 = +$11,640 - $8,925 = +$2,715 This should be VERY profitable! But actual P&L = -$13.48 CONCLUSION: TP and SL almost never trigger. Timeout exit dominates. The price doesn't move 0.40% in 15 min. The OHLCV proxy signals have ZERO directional edge.

1.2 Three Root Causes

RC#1: Signal Death (60% of failure)

v2.0 CDS design: OBI(0.25) + AggressiveFlow(0.30) + MicroPrice(0.20) + VolSpike(0.15) + ATR(0.10)

OHLCV can compute: VolSpike(0.15) + ATR(0.10) = only 25% of signal weight

The 3 core directional signals (75% weight) require L2 orderbook + trade-level data. On OHLCV, they degrade to noise. The remaining Volume+ATR carry no directional information. Result: entries are random → WR converges to ~(SL/(TP+SL)) = 38.5% minus costs.

Actual WR 44.9% > 38.5% suggests the regime filter added ~6% over random, but that alone can't reach 55%.

RC#2: TP 0.40% Unreachable (25% of failure)

Math: BTC 5-min ATR median ≈ $70 = 0.082%. In 15 min (3 bars): expected range = 0.082% × √3 = 0.142%. TP at 0.40% = 2.8σ move = probability <1% per random walk. Even with perfect signal, P(TP hit before SL in 15min) ≈ 30-35% max. The 55% assumption was wrong.

RC#3: Regime Detection Lag (15% of failure)

ADX(14)+Hurst+BBW(20) on 5-min bars has 15-25 min structural lag. During regime transitions (range→trend), 3-5 mean-reversion trades are placed against the emerging trend. These trades have WR <40%, dragging aggregate WR down by 2-3%.

Bottom line: v2.0 was designed for L2+trade data. Running it on OHLCV is like testing sonar in a vacuum. The fix isn't parameter tuning — it's either change the data or change the strategy.

2.3 Dynamic Exit (Core Fix)

ATR_14 = ATR(14, 5-min bars) TP = ATR_14 × 2.0 SL = ATR_14 × 1.5 RR = 1.33 (gross), ~1.15 (net of costs) TIME_STOP = 9 bars (45 min) MID-VOL EXAMPLE (ATR=$70=0.082%): TP = 0.165% ($49.5 on $30k) SL = 0.124% ($37.0 on $30k) WHY 2.0x ATR / 1.5x ATR: Random walk: P(hit +2 ATR before -1.5 ATR) = 1.5/3.5 = 42.9% With directional edge: +5-8% above random = 48-51% TP hit rate SL hit rate: ~32-35% TO rate: ~17-20% This is ACHIEVABLE because targets scale with actual volatility.

2.4 Regime Filter (Simplified)

ADX_10 (faster response than ADX_14) RANGE (trade): ADX_10 < 30 TREND (stop): ADX_10 > 30 Cooldown: 6 bars (30 min) after TREND→RANGE Eliminates Hurst (noisy on OHLCV) and BBW (redundant with ADX) Reduces lag by ~2 bars vs v2.0
