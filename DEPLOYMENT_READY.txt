SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING

Issue: "LLM timeout"
Solution: Increase LLM_CONFIG['timeout'] from 30 to 60 seconds

Issue: "Grid not updating"
Solution: Price must move >20% from grid center
         Check: abs(current_price - grid_center) / grid_range > 0.2

Issue: "No orders generated"
Solution: Verify grid_levels and grid_spacing_pct settings
         Ensure grid is within ±5% of current price
