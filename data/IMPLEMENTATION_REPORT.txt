TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

Position Sizing (Kelly Criterion):
- Formula: fractional_kelly = (bp + p - q) / b × 0.55
- Conservative fraction: 0.55
- Min position: 1% of capital
- Max position: 25% of capital
- Based on: win_rate, expected_return, stop_loss_percent

Risk Management Hierarchy:
1. Daily Loss Limit (-5% of initial capital)
2. Position Size Limit (25% of available capital)
3. Position Stop-Loss (-2.5% from entry)
4. Trailing Stop (0.75% from highest price)
5. Emergency Stop (-10% total drawdown)
